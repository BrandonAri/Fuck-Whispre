## Why this exists

OpenAI released Whisper as an open-source speech recognition model. That means developers can study it, modify it, build with it, and give people access to it without **MONETIZATION** or **CAPITALIZING** on the model itself.

Fuck Whisper exists because people are fucking greedy.

This was vibe coded in an hour to prove that most apps on the market are worth nothing. They’re not putting real effort into the core technology. This is how easy it is to package an open model into an app without charging anything.

Use it exactly like the apps people pay monthly for.

Press a key. Speak. Get text.

## What it does

Fuck Whisper lets you dictate text directly into your computer using OpenAI’s open-source Whisper speech recognition model.

Everything runs offline on your local machine.

No internet required.

## Requirements

- macOS
- Apple Silicon
- Tested on macOS 27.
- Other configurations may work, but have not been tested.
